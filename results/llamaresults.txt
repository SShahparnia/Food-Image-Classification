Breakfast Burrito with Eggs, Greens, and Milk

Ingredients:

* 1 cup cooked eggs (140 calories)
* 1/2 cup cooked greens (20 calories)
* 1/4 cup milk (50 calories)
* 1 tablespoon olive oil (120 calories)
* Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a small non-stick pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
2. Add the cooked eggs and cook until heated through.
3. Add the cooked greens to the pan and cook until wilted.       
4. Pour in the milk and stir to combine.
5. Warm a tortilla in the microwave for 10-15 seconds.
6. Spoon the egg and greens mixture onto the tortilla and roll up.
7. Serve and enjoy!

Total calories: 350
